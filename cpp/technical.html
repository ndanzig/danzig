Some Language Specifications

  • Conditional Operators
    1. || - logical or
    2. | - binary or
    3. && - logical and
    4. & - binary and
    5. ^ - exclusive or ( xor )
    6. != ,     == ,    <=,     >=,    < ,     >,     ! ( condition )
    7. "short circuiting"
  • Operators
    1. +,     -,     =,     *,     /,     %,     ++,     --,     +=,    -=,    *=,    /= ,     %=
    2. condition ? statement : statement ;
    3. i++ vs. ++i
  • Casting.
          Example:
          int a =7;
          cout <<(float)a;
  • int, float, double, long, bool, unsigned, char, short, const
