The 9-Pin Connector

    The pins abbreviations are (numbers in parentheses are the 25D pin numbers).


    1. CD (8)

    2. RD (Rx) (3)

    3. TD (Tx) (2)

    4. DTR (20)

    5. SG (Ground) (7)

    6. DSR (6)

    7. RTS (4)

    8. CTS (5)

    9. RI (22)
