  4. Deadlines:
    • The projects must be submitted by the day indicated before the lab session for that day. See the grading and project instructions for how to submit them.
    • You will be orally tested on the project you turn in (to make sure you really understand it), during one of the following lab sessions:
      1. the session on the day that the project is due, or
      2. the following session.

      This is slightly different for the first and last projects. See the schedule below for details.

      We spread the testing over 2 sessions, because students are required to come to only half the sessions.

      You might have noticed that Dr. Teichtel has circulated among the students during the lab session, while I talked. He'll be testing you this time.

      A few students have received exemptions from coming to lab sessions. These students should schedule a time for me to test them, within a week after the project is due.

    • See the grading and project instructions for how being late or early will affect your grade.
    • Besides being tested on your understanding of your projects, you will also be tested on other subjects. These tests are short.
